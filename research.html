Dr. Wongbong Choi's Battery Lab
Fall 2025 - Present

- Granted a $4,000 scholarship for the summer of 2026 to conduct research on ways to make polymer-based zinc batteries more durable and fire-resistant for industrial and other applications

- Fabricate polymer-based zinc cell pouches to identify optimal material composition

- Created poster to compete in the Fort Worth Regional Science Fair

- Recorded data with X-ray diffractometer, Raman spectroscopy, Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy, Cyclic Voltammetry, Thermogravimetric analysis, Battery Cycler, and Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM).
